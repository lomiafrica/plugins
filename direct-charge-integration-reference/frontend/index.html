lomi Direct Charge Integration Reference

Minimal demo for POST /charge/wave, POST /charge/mtn, and POST /charge/card. Card payments use @lomi./sdk Payment Elements — no raw card data touches your server.

1) Wave direct charge

2) MTN MoMo direct charge

3) Embedded card charge (Payment Element)

Step A: create the charge on your server. Step B: mount the Payment Element with the returned client_secret via @lomi./sdk. Step C: call lomi.confirmPayment().
